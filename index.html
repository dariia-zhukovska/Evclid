    Yes, it is possible. However, our specialists must analyze the finished project for mistakes and implementation possibilities.

  • I'm choosing between different companies. What are your differences?

    Our company has been on the market for over 20 years. The company is distinguished by high professionalism, extensive experience and many successful projects. You can read the impressions of our customers in the "reviews" section.

  • Can I delegate to you work / coordination with a contractor / organization?

    This service is not included in the basic package, but you can delegate the work and coordination with the contractor for an additional fee.

  • Can I get a refund at any stage of the work?

    Yes, you can get a refund at any stage of the job, but if the job lasts more than a month, the 15% deposit you pay at the beginning is non-refundable.
